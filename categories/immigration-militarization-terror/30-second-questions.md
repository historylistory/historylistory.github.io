### Question 1 

President Trump has repeatedly described immigrants in terms of “bad genes,” “poisoning the blood” of the country, and, most recently, genetics unlike those of other Americans. That language suggests that criminality and human worth are inherited traits, an idea historically associated with eugenics. Do you condemn that rhetoric, and what will you do to ensure immigration policy never treats people as biologically inferior because of their ancestry?

### Question 2

This administration claims it is making our cities safer, yet federal immigration agents shot Marimar Martinez after saying, “Do something, bitch,” and later bragged, “I fired five rounds and she had seven holes.” Nicole Good's last words were "It's okay, I'm not mad at you". Alex Pretti's last action was to protect a woman. The last words of the father in Maine who was executed in front of his 3-year-old girl was, "I tried to stop". The death toll has risen so high that even the U.N. has called for an investigation. Do you support this militarization of our streets, and what part of Americans being terrorized by their own government makes us safer?

SOME SOURCES (just the deaths that were in the news, this doesn’t even include the injuries and near-deaths) 

* UN INVESTIGATION June 26, 2026 The death count has gotten so high the U.N. is calling for an investigation into it [UN.org](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/06/1167816)

* DEATH March 1, 2025 ICE agents killed 23-year-old Ruben Ray Martinez and video contradicted ICE claims that he tried to hit them with his vehicle x[PBS.org](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/show/he-took-my-sons-life-for-nothing-says-mother-of-man-killed-by-immigration-agent)

* DEATH September 12, 2025 Silverio Villegas Gonzalez. DHS claimed he weaponized his car, but video footage contradicted their claims [Reuters.com](https://www.reuters.com/world/ice-officer-kills-man-chicago-suburb-during-arrest-attempt-2025-09-12/)

* DEATH December 31, 2025 Keith Porter. DHS alleged Porter fired an AR15 at them.  [Word in Black.com](https://wordinblack.com/2026/01/ice-agent-killed-black-man-los-angeles-why-no-arrest/)

* DEATH January 7, 2026 Renee Nicole Good. Her last words were “It’s okay, I’m not mad at you” and the DOJ blocked Minnesota law officials from investigating the scene. Afterwards the DOJ harassed Good’s spouse [The Guardian.com](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/jan/09/ice-agent-minneapolis-bodycam-footage)

* DEATH January 23, 2026 Alex Pretti. They shot him several times in the back while he was held face-down. The DOJ blocked Minnesota law officials from investigating the scene. [NPR.org](https://www.npr.org/2026/01/25/nx-s1-5687875/minneapolis-shooting-minnesota-ice-alex-pretti-dhs-investigation)

* DEATH February 26, 2026 Nurul Amim Shah Alam. ICE left him alone miles from his home and didn’t contact his family. He was found dead outside in the cold. [Investigative Post.org](https://www.investigativepost.org/2026/02/25/blind-refugee-abandoned-by-border-patrol-is-dead/)

* DEATH July 7, 2026 Agents chased down Lorenzo Salgado Araujo, shot him in his car, then dragged his lifeless body out of the vehicle. The three witnesses were deported. [CNN.com](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/09/us/lorenzo-salgado-araujo-houston-ice-shooting)
