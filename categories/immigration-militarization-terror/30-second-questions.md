### Question 1 

President Trump has repeatedly described immigrants in terms of “bad genes,” “poisoning the blood” of the country, and, most recently, genetics unlike those of other Americans. That language suggests that criminality and human worth are inherited traits, an idea historically associated with eugenics. Do you condemn that rhetoric, and what will you do to ensure immigration policy never treats people as biologically inferior because of their ancestry?
