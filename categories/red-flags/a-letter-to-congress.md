### SAMPLE LETTER

Dear Sir or Madam,

Some actions taken by this administration are more than frustrating—they are deeply unsettling. There are too many parallels to the tactics used by authoritarian governments throughout history to simply dismiss them as politics. The United States has made serious mistakes before, but we are now increasingly being viewed as the aggressor rather than the defender of democratic values.

Consider what has already occurred. I have not even included media suppression, retaliation against protesters and universities, voter intimidation, or the use of military forces in ways that raise serious Posse Comitatus concerns. Those issues alone deserve their own discussion. What follows are additional examples that should alarm anyone who values constitutional government and the rule of law.

Taken together, these actions reflect a pattern of consolidating power, weakening democratic institutions, and normalizing conduct that would have been unacceptable in any previous administration. Regardless of political affiliation, that should concern every American.

Look at these examples and ask yourself whether they represent the principles our country was founded upon. Ask whether this is the direction we want our nation to continue moving. If the answer is no, then we should be working together to oppose what increasingly resembles an authoritarian government rather than dismissing each incident as an isolated event.
