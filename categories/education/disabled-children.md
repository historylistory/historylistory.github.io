### 2025 September

2025-09-08 Multiple news articles cite Trump paused funding for special education programs including special education teacher training. One article quoted the U.S. Department of Education as saying this department is "inconsistent with, and no longer effectuates, the best interests of the federal government", further citing that organizations were only given one week to submit an appeal.
