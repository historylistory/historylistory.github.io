Programs designed to help disadvantaged and historically marginalized communities are being dismantled, while efforts to erase or rewrite difficult chapters of our nation's history continue.

Public officials have faced little or no accountability for sharing blatantly racist memes. These are not isolated incidents—they represent a pattern of legitimizing and empowering extremist ideology.

How can you support a political movement that repeatedly enables and elevates white supremacist ideology? The following outlines my concerns in greater detail. Some examples are included to provide historical context and demonstrate how these patterns have developed over time.
