### Question 1 

President Trump complained that the Smithsonian focuses on “how bad slavery was,” ordered his attorneys to scrutinize museums as they had universities, and directed federal sites to remove or revise exhibits concerning slavery and Native American history. Do you support politicians pressuring museums and national parks to present a sanitized version of our past, and will you defend historians’ ability to teach the complete truth, including America’s injustices?
