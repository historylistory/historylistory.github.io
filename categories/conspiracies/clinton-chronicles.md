### 1994

1994-01-01 Televangelist Jerry Falwell stirred up conspiracy mongering when he produced and promoted The Clinton Chronicles, a documentary (or mockumentary) that accused Bill Clinton of crimes of drug addiction, affairs and harassment, misuse of state funds, money laundering, profiting from drug smuggling, witness murders, cover ups of suspicious deaths, and destruction of incriminating documents.
