### 2025 January

2025-01-27 In 2017 Trump initiated efforts to ban transgender people from serving the Armed forces and the policy took place 2019. President Joe Biden reversed the order in 2021, but Trump once again signed a new executive order in 2025 to reinstate and expand the ban. A federal judge blocked Trump’s effort to ban transgender troops from military service in March, but the Supreme Court inevitably allowed the ban to proceed.

### 2025 June

Hegseth wrote on social media, “I am pleased to announce that the United States Navy is renaming the USNS Harvey Milk to the USNS Oscar V. Peterson. We are taking the politics out of ship naming”.
