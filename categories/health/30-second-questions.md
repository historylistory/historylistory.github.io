### Question 1 

Canada’s health minister has warned that U.S. health and science institutions can no longer be relied upon for accurate information, and the Canadian Medical Association reports growing concern about American health misinformation. How does it reflect on our country when a close ally no longer trusts our public-health agencies, and what will you do to restore credibility and public confidence at the Department of Health and Human Services?
