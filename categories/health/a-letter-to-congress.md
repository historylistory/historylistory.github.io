### SAMPLE LETTER

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to express my deep concern about what I view as the Trump administration's rhetoric and policies toward disabled Americans, seniors, veterans, and other vulnerable populations. Actions such as cutting the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP), imposing enrollment moratoriums for medical equipment and supplies, reducing nursing positions, and interfering with end-of-life care decisions have raised serious concerns about the administration's priorities.

I am also troubled by efforts to defund or oppose legislation intended to assist veterans, people with disabilities, women's reproductive rights, and individuals experiencing homelessness. Repeated proposals involving the privatization of Social Security, negative portrayals of Social Security beneficiaries, and discussions about raising the retirement age further contribute to these concerns. Additionally, the administration has, at times, dismissed accessibility initiatives as being too "woke," which many view as disregarding the needs of people with disabilities.

Taken together, these actions and statements suggest a lack of compassion and respect for some of the nation's most vulnerable citizens. In my view, effective leadership should prioritize the well-being and dignity of all Americans. The examples below illustrate the basis for my concerns and frustration. Some are included primarily to provide context for the broader pattern I believe they represent.
