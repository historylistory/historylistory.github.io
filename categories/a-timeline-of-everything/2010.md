### 2010

This is the year podcasts increasingly referenced **the Great Replacement theory** in extremist circles like the 2017 "Unite the Right" rally. As an example of how dangerous this conspiracy is, the white supremacist responsible for the Buffalo NY massacre in 2022 bought into the great replacement theory; **it was found in his manifesto**.

During a Libertopia conference, Palantir co-founder and Trump mega-donor Peter Thiel explained **how big tech companies have a hand in taking over the vote**.
