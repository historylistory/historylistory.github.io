### Question 1 

Secretary Hegseth previously said women should not serve in combat roles, eliminated the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services, and has removed or blocked prominent women from senior military leadership. Yet women have fought, sacrificed, and died for this country. What do you tell female service members who see these actions as a message that their service is less valued, and will you publicly oppose policies that limit their opportunities based on gender rather than performance?
