### 2025 January

**I am sure there are several more Executive Orders pertaining to or affecting women’s rights, but here are a few listed on the White House website of rescissions the Trump Administration listed as “harmful”.**

2025-01-20 Trump rescinded Executive Order 13988 Preventing and Combating Discrimination on the Basis of Gender Identity or Sexual Orientation.

[https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/)

2025-01-20 Trump rescinded Executive Order 14020 Establishment of the White House Gender Policy Council.

[https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/)

2025-01-20 Trump rescinded Executive Order 14021 Guaranteeing an Educational Environment Free From Discrimination on the Basis of Sex, Including Sexual Orientation or Gender Identity.

[https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/initial-rescissions-of-harmful-executive-orders-and-actions/)
