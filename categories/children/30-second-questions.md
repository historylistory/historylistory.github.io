### Question 1

The administration boasts that tariffs are generating revenue and policies from the Big Beautiful Bill are saving taxpayers money, yet this administration claims America cannot afford broader childcare assistance and has taken SNAP benefits. Meanwhile, taxpayers paid for a costly retrofit of a Qatari luxury jet, a $220 million advertising campaign for Kristi Noem, financial support for Argentina, and potentially enormous settlements to cancel offshore wind projects. Why are these priorities affordable, but helping American families afford childcare or raise their children is not?
