### 2015

2015-08-03 (context) After the Paris Climate Agreement conference, Obama initiated the Clean Power Plan. For context, the US is responsible for almost 20% of historical cumulative emissions.

### 2017

2017-06-01 Trump withdrew the U.S. from the Paris Climate Accord.

[https://globalaffairs.org/research/public-opinion-survey/despite-us-withdrawal-paris-agreement-majority-still-supports-it](https://globalaffairs.org/research/public-opinion-survey/despite-us-withdrawal-paris-agreement-majority-still-supports-it)

### 2025 January

2025-01-20 Project 2025, page 709 outlines intentions to withdraw from the UN Paris Agreement and UN Framework Convention on Climate Change. On page 366 the mandate – written by the Heritage Foundation – complains about subsidies for clean energy.

[https://static.heritage.org/project2025/2025_MandateForLeadership_FULL.pdf](https://static.heritage.org/project2025/2025_MandateForLeadership_FULL.pdf)

2025-01-20 Trump withdrew the U.S. from the Paris Climate Accord a second time.
