### Question 1 

The administration is pushing AI into schools, weakening regulatory safeguards, seeking to override state protections, and deploying Grok within the Pentagon. Meanwhile, AI systems have produced sexualized images, spread dangerous misinformation, exposed children to explicit content, and given them instructions involving weapons and self-harm. Will you demand enforceable regulation and safety standards before allowing AI integration in our schools, or are our children being used as test subjects for an inadequately regulated industry?
