Meanwhile, AI systems have produced sexualized images, spread dangerous misinformation, exposed children to explicit content, and given them instructions involving weapons and self-harm. Will you demand enforceable regulation and safety standards before allowing AI integration in our schools, or are our children being used as test subjects for an inadequately regulated industry?
