### Trump Shutdowns

As of March 2026 there were two shutdowns in Trump’s first term, and so far two in the second term. 

The October 2025 shutdown was because the **GOP did not want to continue affordable healthcare for Americans**, and the February 2026 shutdown was because **the GOP didn’t want guardrails placed on ICE agents to prevent further harm to or executions of American citizens.**
