### Question 1 

President Trump previously proposed cutting the Transportation Department’s discretionary budget by roughly 13 percent, and in 2025 his administration fired hundreds of FAA employees, including workers who supported aviation safety and maintenance. It also proposed cutting TSA funding while our controller workforce remains thousands below targeted staffing and our air-traffic systems urgently need modernization. Why does this administration keep targeting the workforce and agencies responsible for safe air travel, and will you oppose any further staffing or safety cuts?
