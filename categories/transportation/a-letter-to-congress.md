### SAMPLE LETTER

Dear Member of Congress,

I am writing to express my deep frustration with the Trump administration’s repeated cuts to transportation funding, reductions in the federal safety workforce, and efforts to privatize essential public services such as air traffic control and TSA operations.

America’s transportation system is already under enormous strain. Recent years have brought fatal plane crashes, runway incidents, near-collisions, equipment outages, staffing shortages, and other alarming safety events. While no single policy explains every incident, cutting FAA, NHTSA, NIOSH, TSA, infrastructure, and aviation-service funding during such a period is reckless.

The history of firing and understaffing transportation workers—from the dismissal of more than 11,000 air traffic controllers in 1981 to recent workforce reductions—should remind us that safety depends on experienced, well-trained, properly supported public employees. Privatization and political loyalty tests are not substitutes for investment, oversight, and professional expertise.

I urge Congress to reject further transportation cuts and privatization proposals, restore canceled safety and infrastructure funding, protect transportation workers, and conduct rigorous oversight of the Department of Transportation and FAA. Americans should not have to wonder whether political agendas and budget cuts are compromising their safety.

Please put public safety ahead of privatization, retaliation, and political theater.
