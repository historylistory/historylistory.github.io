### 2026 April

2026-04-28 (for context, because we need more nurses) Senator Merkley pressed DOE Linda McMahon on why she gutted the TRIO program designed to encourage professional nursing careers. McMahon claimed that somehow removing nursing jobs from a professional category would make tuition more affordable. What actually happens is students who can’t get a sufficient federal loan on a low interest rate will now have to seek out PRIVATIZED loan companies with a much higher interest rate. Remember, in November 2025 the DOE declassified nursing careers from being considered professional.
