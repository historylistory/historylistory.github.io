### 1949

04-04-1949 NATO was created to promote collective security during the Cold War era, to deter the spread of the Soviet Union, to prevent countries from being nationalistic or militaristic over other countries, and to adhere to Democratic values. It has also been:

- Cold War: as a “nuclear umbrella”

- Post Cold War Era (1989): for “peacemaking”

- Post 9/11 (2001): for “counterterrorism” (with vague definitions of terrorism) ***NATO invoked Article 5
